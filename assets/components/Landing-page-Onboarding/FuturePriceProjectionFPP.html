Future Price Projection (FPP)

The Investable score represents how good the property is for the investment. A higher score means higher return probabilities in the project.

It measures current price productivity, which means if it is fairly priced, under-priced, or an overpriced property. And what are the property's prospects for growth and future price appreciation, based on how the future infrastructure connectivity in the economy influences the property.

It also measures the execution risk in the property that represents the probabilities of delay and defaults in the timely delivery of the project based on developers' and projects' performance ratings.
